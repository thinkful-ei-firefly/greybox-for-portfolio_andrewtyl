About Me

Hi, my name is Andrew Jessen-Tyler, and I am a Full Stack Web Devloper and Programmer eager to leave a mark on the tech industry. I've always loved technology. Passion has driven my life, and my passion floureshes when it comes to programming. I want to leave a mark behind on the world. Whether that be Artificial Intelligence, Immersive Virtual Reality, new mental health treatments, or something yet to be discovered, I want to be able to better people's lives in some way. Outside of programming, I like to throw myself into new worlds. Sometimes that's with tv series or movies, but typically its through gaming. One of the greatest feelings in the world is feeling like the character you are playing as, when button presses aren't "I need to press this button to do this", but rather "this muscle makes me do this in the game." When not immersing myself in new worlds, I tend to watch videos on Youtube. Some just plain "let's plays", some comedy sketches, some educational, and some critiques of movies and games.
